Projects

Hateful Meme Detection in Social Media Platforms

I, along with my team members, compared the performance of DL architectures such as MultiModal Bitranformers and Concat-Bert for hateful meme detection. We qualitatively studied the shortcomings of these architectures by analyzing GRADCAM heatmaps of false-negative and false-positive samples. We used PCA to obtain most salient image features and employed ensembling techniques to combine the advantages of various DL models, achieving a significant improvement in the accuracy of baseline architectures.

COVID-19 infected more than 10,000 people in South Korea. In this project, We employed DS4C South Korea Patient dataset which is created using the information announced by Korea Centers for Disease Control & Prevention and built the contact trace to determine the likely infected people which were not reported using SoTA algorithms.

Paraphase Question Generation Using Graph Convolutional Networks

In this project, we propose an encoder-decoder model to create a better sentence level embedding and evaluate the model on the task of Paraphrase Question generation. The semantics are captured by a pairwise decoder that enforces encodings of similar sentences to be close to each other. Further, the syntactics of sentences are captured by stacking a GCN over LSTM states.
